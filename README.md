# Blind Opportunity Detector üöÄ

## üìå Problem Statement
Students often miss out on important opportunities such as internships, jobs, and scholarships due to lack of timely information or awareness. This leads to lost chances that could have a significant impact on their careers.

## üí° Solution
Blind Opportunity Detector is an *interactive dashboard* that analyzes opportunity details and identifies:
- Students who are *alerted (matched)* for the opportunity
- Students who *missed* the opportunity

The system provides *real-time insights* using metrics, charts, and visual cards to clearly highlight opportunity gaps.

---

## üéØ Key Features
- üìä *Dashboard Metrics*
  - Total opportunities
  - Total alerted (matched) students
  - Total missed students

- üìà *Visual Analytics*
  - Donut chart for *Matched vs Missed students*
  - Bar chart for *students by opportunity category*

- üìù *Multi-line Opportunity Submission*
  - Paste multiple opportunities at once (one per line)

- üîç *Search & Filter*
  - Quickly find opportunities using keywords

- üåô *Dark Mode*
  - Improved accessibility and user experience

- üì§ *Export to CSV*
  - Download opportunity data for reporting and analysis

- üë• *Expandable Student Lists*
  - View alerted and missed students per opportunity
